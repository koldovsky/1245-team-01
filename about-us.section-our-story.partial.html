our story

EchoWave Radio is your favorite wave in New York. Thousands of citizens listen to our broadcasts, playlists, and podcasts while they commute, work, cook, love, and simply live.

At EchoWave, we believe in the extraordinary power of music to transcend boundaries, evoke emotions, and create lasting connections. Our journey began in New York in 2005, when our owner, Jack Richardson, met other music enthusiasts with a shared vision: to redefine the auditory experience. Today, EchoWave stands as a beacon of musical diversity, a community united by the love for extraordinary sounds. Reflect with us on our history, filled with gratitude for each note, each beat, and each listener who has been a part of this incredible journey. Here's to the years gone by and the countless beats yet to be discovered. Thank you for being part of our musical evolution.
